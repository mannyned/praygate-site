Features

The Gate

A moment of pause before you scroll

When you try to open a blocked app, PrayGate doesn't just say no. It creates a gentle, intentional pause that invites you to reconsider.

  1. 1

    Hold to unlock

    A three-second press gives you time to pause and think before continuing.

  2. 2

    Reflect for a moment

    A short countdown with a daily scripture reminds you why you set this boundary.

  3. 3

    Choose intentionally

    Proceed if you truly need to, or choose to begin a prayer session instead.
